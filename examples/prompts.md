# Example Prompts

This file contains short, paraphrased prompt examples adapted from the public project page. They are useful as placeholders for future demos, tests, or generation scripts.

## Multi-Subject Interaction

- A boy crouches in a cherry blossom forest and offers dried fish to a cautious white cat while petals fall around them.
- In a living room, a dog leaps onto a beige sofa while carrying a rabbit doll and then settles near the armrest.

## Identity-Preserving Generation

- On a stadium pitch, a man struggles to steady himself on a broomstick while a golden flying ball darts around his head.
- In a European-style alley, a man in a gray sweatshirt lifts a slice of bread and interacts with nearby objects.

## Comparison-Style Cases

- A hedgehog on a lawn covered with autumn leaves nudges an acorn under warm, orange light.
- A cockatoo swoops down toward a piece of bread held by a person in a sweatshirt.
- In a Martian desert scene, a suited figure examines a reddish-brown rock while red sand reflects in the visor.
- In a room, a woman wearing pink headphones closes her eyes and smiles while listening to music.
